What to read to understand poker, the finest game ever invented

16 April 2023

A thrilling account of a shipwreck in the Pacific in 1741

13 April 2023

"The Wager" by David Grann is a gripping non-fiction account of a British frigate that crashed onto rocks off the coast of Patagonia while pursuing the enemy into the Pacific in 1741. The story revolves around three complex figures, the captain, David Cheap, John Bulkeley, a working seaman, and John Byron, a midshipman, and their power struggle as they become castaways on a small island in the Golfo de Penas. The marooned crew feuded over where to assign fault and how to get home, and the privations that the men endured over the following months and years are almost unimaginable.
