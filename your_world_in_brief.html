America’s economic outperformance is a marvel to behold

13 April 2023

Despite concerns from both the left and right about America's economic decline, the country's dominance in the global economy remains striking. While China's economy has surpassed America's in terms of purchasing power, America's pre-eminence is clear when looking at exchange rates set by markets. America's outperformance has translated into wealth for its people, with income per person in America being higher than in Western Europe and Japan. However, America's extreme volatility and lack of investment in social spending have led to problems such as high unemployment and a declining life expectancy, and potential self-sabotage policies threaten to undermine its success.

Money Stuff: The SEC Comes for Bittrex

09 December 2020

The US Securities Exchange Commission (SEC) has charged Bittrex and its former CEO with operating an unregistered national securities exchange, broker and clearing agency. The SEC alleges that Bittrex facilitated the buying and selling of crypto assets that it considered securities, earning at least $1.3bn in revenues from transaction fees. US regulators are considering whether to require cryptocurrency exchanges to register with the SEC, reflecting the SEC's record of regulatory failure. The SEC is also considering whether to tweak existing rules to allow firms to offer innovative ways of financing using new technologies. The report also discusses interest-rate bets made by Wall Street professionals, the business models of retail stock brokerages, and a lawsuit against Fox Corp. over its coverage of the US election.
